Tips on Searching the Securities Lawyer's Deskbook

  1. If you would like to search the Securities Lawyer's Deskbook, the link below will bring you to search engine that searches the entire University of Cincinnati College of Law website. This will produce results that link to all relevant web pages in the Securities Lawyer's Deskbook, but may also include some links to College of Law web pages outside of the Securities Lawyer's Deskbook.

  2. If you wish to perform a search, type the word(s) for which you want to search in the box of the search page, leaving the settings as they are, and clicking the "search" button. This should produce acceptable results for most purposes. If you are somewhat familiar with search engine options you can take advantage of the pull down menus to vary your search. Most users, however, will get acceptable results without changing the default settings.

  3. You should use the Forward and Back buttons on your browser to navigate between the Results page, the Deskbookpages and the Search page.
