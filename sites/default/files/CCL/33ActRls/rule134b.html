Rule 134b -- Statements of Additional Information


For the purpose only of Section 5(b) of the Act, the term "prospectus" as defined in Section 2(a)(10) of the Act does not include a Statement of Additional Information filed as part of a registration statement on Form N-1A (§ 239.15A and § 274.11A of this chapter), Form N-2 (§ 239.14 and § 274.11a-1 of this chapter), Form N-3 (§ 239.17a and § 274.11b of this chapter), Form N-4 (§ 239.17b and § 274.11c of this chapter), or Form N-6 (§ 239.17c and § 274.11d of this chapter) transmitted prior to the effective date of the registration statement if it is accompanied or preceded by a preliminary prospectus meeting the requirements of Rule 430.

Regulatory History


57 FR 56834, Dec. 1, 1992; 67 FR 19848, 19868, Apr. 23, 2002.
