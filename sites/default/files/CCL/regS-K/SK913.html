Standard Instructions for Filing Forms under
the Securities Act of 1933, Securities Exchange Act of 1934, and Energy Policy and Conservation Act of 1975

Regulation S-K





Item 913 -- Other Provisions of the Transaction


  1. State whether or not appraisal rights are provided under applicable state law, under the partnership's governing instruments or will be voluntarily accorded by the successor, the general partner or the sponsor (or any of their affiliates) in connection with the roll-up transaction. If so, summarize such appraisal rights. If appraisal rights will not be available to investors who object to the transaction, briefly outline the rights which may be available to such investors under such law.

  2. If any provision has been made to allow investors to obtain access to the books and records of the partnership or to obtain counsel or appraisal services at the expense of the successor, the general partner, the partnership, the sponsor (or any of their affiliates), describe such provision.

  3. Discuss the investors' rights under federal and state law to obtain a partnership's list of investors.

Regulatory History


56 FR 57247, Nov. 8, 1991
