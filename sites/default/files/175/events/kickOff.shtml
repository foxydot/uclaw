175th Anniversary Kick-off Celebration (9.28.07)

A familiar old figure not seen on campus in more than 75 years returns to the University of Cincinnati for the kick-off celebration of the 175th anniversary of the UC College of Law!

See President Taft, a graduate and former dean of the College, joined on the platform by UC President Nancy Zimpher and law dean Louis Bilionis, as the sparklers are lit, the Bearcat goes wild, and the UC pep band strikes up a chorus of Happy Birthday for the country's fourth oldest continuously operating law school.
