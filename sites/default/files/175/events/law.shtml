Law & Literature Symposium (2/29)

Lawyer-poet Professor Lawrence Joseph, St. John’s University School of Law, was the key speaker at this event. An internationally acclaimed poet and attorney, he has been named “the most important lawyer-poet of our era.” Professor Joseph’s work has received wide spread attention and praise. From Lawyerland, currently being adapted into a film, to books on labor, employment, tort and compensation law, Professor Joseph brings a unique perspective to his work.
