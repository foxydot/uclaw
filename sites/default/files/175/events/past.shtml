A Look Back At Previous Events

Throughout the 2007/2008 academic year, the College of Law will host numerous events, such as Alumni Reunion Weekend, the Order of the Coif Distinguished Visitor Program, and our 175th Anniversary Gala. If you can't attend an event, or if you want to relive the experience, we have you covered. Check back regularly to view scenes from the festivities, check out video, and read what your colleagues and peers are saying. We know you will enjoy this section. Besides, you never know who you'll see!

Did you take photos at an event? Perhaps you recorded it?  If you documented an event, we hope you'll share it with your friends. Please send your photos, testimonials and/or YouTube videos to counselor@uc.edu. Your work may be posted online and your peers will see things from your perspective. What better way to share?
