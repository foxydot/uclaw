    Faculty News Archives

    Faculty News is a publication highlighting the activities and accomplishments of the University of Cincinnati College of Law faculty.

    Faculty News is edited by Paul Caron, Charles Hartsock Professor of Law and Director of Faculty Projects. Faculty News is publised monthly (except during the summer).

    Comments and suggestions are welcome. Please contact Professor Caron by email at paul.caron@uc.edu. To contact individual faculty members, please visit the Faculty Directory.
