Corporate Law Center Hosts November Conference on Counsel's Role During Economic Crisis

The Corporate Law Center, along with the Association of Corporate Counsel-Southwest Ohio Chapter, presents “The Role of Corporate Counsel During a Time of Economic Crisis” on November 19, 2009. College of Business Tax Conference Offers CLEs for Lawyers
