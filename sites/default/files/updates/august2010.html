Meet the Class of 2013

School has begun for the College of Law's 144 first-year students. On August 16, 2010 the Class of 2013 started their academic careers with "Intro to Law," the week-long orientation program designed to acclimate students to the law school environment. This year's incoming class is an impressive, diverse group. Boasting a median LSAT score of 161, they range in age from 21 to 56. Sixty-one percent of the class is male, while 39 percent is female. This year, almost 20 percent of the class is a minority. (Read more)

You've Been Served: A Day of Service and Community Commitment

On September 11, a day of collective remembrance and national service, students, faculty, staff, and alumni at the University of Cincinnati College of Law will join together to give back to the Greater Cincinnati community.
