Admissions Department Launches Alumni Program

The Admissions Department is kicking off a new program where alumni across the country can help promote UC Law. Alumni will be asked to contact selected admitted applicants in their area to share their UC Law experience, as well as discuss career opportunities for grads. Contact the Admissions team at admissions@law.uc.edu to learn more.

G. Edward White Lectures on the Framer's Constitution at the 2008 Taft Lecture

Traditionally, the foundational ideas about our country have included concepts of liberty, equality, protection of private property, popular sovereignty, federalism, and separation of powers. But what happens when these ideas are re-examined through a prism of the defining events of the founding period? Perhaps some of the ideas aren’t as closely connected as once thought. Join Professor White on October 7 for a presentation on this thought-provoking topic. (Read more)
