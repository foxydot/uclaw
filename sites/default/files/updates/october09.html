Corporate Law Center Hosts November Conference on Counsel's Role During Economic Crisis

The Corporate Law Center, along with the Association of Corporate Counsel-Southwest Ohio Chapter, presents “The Role of Corporate Counsel During a Time of Economic Crisis” on November 19, 2009. This event includes a presentation on the proactive measures corporate counsel can take in a distressed debtor/bankruptcy situation, as well as a panel discussion with lawyers from area corporations and law firms. (Read more)

College of Business Tax Conference Offers CLEs for Lawyers

Participate in the 42nd Annual Tax Conference for tax practitioners, attorneys, paralegals and more.  Presented by the UC College of Business, this event will include an update on the latest tax laws as well as tax preparation guidance. Lawyers can receive 13 CLE credits.  Held December 15-16, 2009, conference speakers include industry leader Dr. Dale Kiefer, Professor Emeritus at the COB. (Read more)
