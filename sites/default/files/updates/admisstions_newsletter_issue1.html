  Message from the Dean

We are very pleased to present to you the inaugural issue of AdmissionseNews, our bulletin designed to update you about Cincinnati Law’s students, alumni, and faculty. Look for up-to-the-minute Admissions information to guide you as you make your law school decision. Learn more about our students and the opportunities provided to them at Cincinnati Law. Get a front-row seat as our alumni take you on a journey through their careers. Then, find out how you can have the same experience, too.  Enjoy!
